Fried Colocasia

Ingredients

500 grams Colocasia
Refined oil for frying
Salt and Spice mix for taste.

Step 1

Wash and boil colocasia in a pressure cooker (three whistles on high and one on low).

Step 2

Allow the colocasia to cool and then peel off the skin.

Step 3

Take a boiled piece and put between a sheet of cellophane paper and slowly press with the round of your palm, so that the colocasia becomes flat. You will get an irregular shaped patty.

Step 4

Heat oil in a shallow pan and while the oil is heating up flatten all the boiled colocasia pieces.

Step 5

Slowly, place the flattened colocasia into the oil and deep fry till you get the golden-red colored fried pieces.

Step 6

Take out from the oil and garnish with a mixture of coriander powder, chili powder and salt as per taste.

Serve hot as a Snack or Appetizer!
