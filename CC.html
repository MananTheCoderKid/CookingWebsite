Coffee Cake

Ingredients

1 ½ cups Plain / All purpose flour (sieved)
2.5 tablespoons of coffee granules dissolved in ¼ cup hot milk
½ teaspoons baking powder
¼ teaspoon baking soda
¼ teaspoon salt
½ cup clarified butter
¾ cup regular sugar
⅔ cup milk or curd
2 Large eggs (lightly beaten)

Step 1

Preheat oven to 170℃ (338℉).

Step 2

Grease and line your baking tin.

Step 3

Sieve the flour and add the salt and baking powder.

Step 4

Cream the butter and sugar until a pale light color.

Step 5

Slowly add beaten eggs to the mixer, on a low-speed setting, a bit at a time.

Step 6

Keep the mixer on a slow speed, add the coffee mixture, and milk (or curd).

Step 7

Transfer the cake mixture to the greased and lined loaf tin.

Step 8

Bake for 40 minutes at 170℃ (338℉).
